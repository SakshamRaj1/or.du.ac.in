About the Department


Operational Research (OR) as a subject was introduced in the University of Delhi in 1963 with the introduction of a two-year postgraduate programme. A decade later in 1973, the Department of Operational Research was independently set up under the Faculty of Mathematical Sciences. It is the first department in India to start a postgraduate programme in Operational Research. Operational Research focuses on advancing Operational Research/Management Science (OR/MS) and Analytics through theoretical insights, methodological developments, and real-world case studies.


Know More

Latest Research


The Department of Operational Research at the University of Delhi (DU) is actively involved in a wide spectrum of cutting-edge research, driving both theoretical advancements and real-world applications. The department is renowned for its focus on mathematical modeling, optimization techniques, and decision-making frameworks, contributing significantly to the discipline of Operational Research. Faculty and scholars are currently engaged in multiple ongoing research projects that address contemporary challenges across sectors. Notable domains include Supply Chain Optimization, Game Theory Applications, and Simulation Modelling, each aimed at enhancing system efficiency, strategic planning, and predictive analytics. These initiatives reflect the department‚Äôs commitment to fostering innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and research excellence.

Highlights

  • Established Legacy: Founded in 1963, the department is a pioneer in Operational Research, under the Faculty of Mathematical Sciences, and part of NAAC A+ accredited University of Delhi.
  • Contemporary Curriculum: The program integrates optimization, simulation, machine learning, forecasting, and data analytics to meet modern industry demands.
  • Faculty & Research Excellence: Nationally and internationally recognized faculty lead active research in Supply Chain, Game Theory, and Simulation Modelling.
  • Practical Industry Exposure: Students apply learned tools in real-world sectors like finance, telecom, and public policy through mandatory industry-linked projects.
  • Career & Alumni Success: Alumni thrive globally at top firms like Deloitte, EY, Mastercard, backed by placement support, skill training, and career counseling.
